  • 1940 - He was born in San Pedro Sucre, a secluded place, a cotton town.
  • 1952 - He had a childhood of much struggle, at the age of twelve she lost his older sister to a strange disease. Now she must begin to support the housework and she is taken to the farms to work.
  • 1957 - At the age of 17, even though he was not of legal age, he had his first son, which he named Fernando Davila, next to his first love, Luis Eduardo Davila.
  • 1961 - He changes city, she arrives to Sincelejo where she has his second son, named Reinaldo.
  • 1972 - She has a third child, this time it is a girl her name is Doris.
  • 1975 - He lives a life of a lot of work, he started his career as a merchant in the city of Sincelejo. As her children grow, she works hard to have a home and feed her family, she emphasizes all the time that education is her best tool, that is why she does not doubt at any time to send all her children to university.
  • 1983 - An unexpected news arrives, his eldest son will have a son, to give him a better future he decides to leave that city and move to a more metropolitan one, his next stop is Cartagena.
  • 1984 - His second great love is born, Luis Davila, to whom I swear to give everything and make him a great person.
  • 2020 - 36 years have passed since then, Eva dedicated her entire life to providing the best education for her three children and her two grandchildren. Today she is sick with Alzheimer's disease and she can remember very little, however we who live with her will always remember her as the warrior, brave and brave woman that she is hers.
